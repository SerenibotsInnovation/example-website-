About Us


Welcome to Serenibots, where innovation and compassion converge to revolutionize healthcare. Our mission is to create advanced robotic solutions that enhance patient care, improve medical outcomes, and support healthcare professionals. From surgical assistants and telemedicine robots to rehabilitation and diagnostic systems, our cutting-edge technology leverages AI and robotics to perform medical tasks with unparalleled precision and reliability. Committed to integrity, innovation, and patient well-being, Serenibots is dedicated to shaping a future where accessible, efficient, and compassionate healthcare is a reality for all. Join us on our journey to transform medicine, one bot at a time.

Our Services

Hospitals




LUNA, our AI-powered healthcare assistant, provides comprehensive patient monitoring, automated diagnostics, and personalized treatment plans. LUNA adapts its personality based on the patient's age, ensuring empathetic interactions while enhancing healthcare efficiency and effectiveness.

Schools / Colleges

LUNA, our AI-powered healthcare assistant, offers regular health check-ups, mental health support, and preventive health education. LUNA adapts its personality based on age, ensuring relatable and engaging interactions, while promoting a healthier and more supportive learning environment.

Health Campaigns

LUNA, our AI-powered healthcare assistant, brings quality care to remote health campaigns with real-time diagnostics, personalized treatments, and empathetic interactions. Tailored to diverse age groups, LUNA enhances healthcare accessibility and outcomes in underserved areas.
